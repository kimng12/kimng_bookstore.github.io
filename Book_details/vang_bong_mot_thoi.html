Vang Bong Mot Thoi

Nguyen Tuan wrote "Vang bong mot thoi" in 1940 - before August Revolution. In fact, this collection of short stories is a compilation of articles from the column "Vang va bong mot thoi" that he published in an old newspaper. Later, it was published by Tan Dan publishing house. This work is considered to be close to perfection, making Nguyen Tuan a shining star in the literary world.

The work consists of 12 short stories that can be divided into two categories. The first type includes stories such as "Nhung chiec am dat", "Huong Cuoi", "Chen tra trong suong som", "Tha tho", "Danh tho", "Mot canh thu muon"... which have a gentle and sophisticated style. They describe the noble and elegant pastimes of ancient scholars during the transition period of Chinese and Western studies. In addition, there are stories like "Chem treo nganh", "Chu nguoi tu tu", "Danh but chi", "Bao oan", which have a thrilling atmosphere that makes readers follow each character with suspense, then admire and respect those underprivileged people with noble hearts or rare talents.
